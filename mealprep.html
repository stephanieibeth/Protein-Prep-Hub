Meal Prep

How to plan a meal prep that works for your goals.

1. Pick your main protein

Chicken, turkey, tofu, salmon, beans, etc. Keep it simple.

2. Add easy sides

Rice, potatoes, pasta, frozen veggies, salad kits.

3. Portion for your week

Decide how many meals you need before you start cooking.

Storage & Reheat Guide

  • Fridge: 3–4 days for most cooked proteins.
  • Freezer: up to 2–3 months (label containers).
  • Reheat: microwave with a splash of water to keep moisture.
Time Saver: Cook once, eat 3–4 times.
Budget Tip: Buy protein in bulk and freeze portions.
