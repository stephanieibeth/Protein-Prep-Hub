Protein Guide

Beginner-friendly help for hitting protein goals.

How much protein do you need?

A simple starting rule: include a protein source in every meal. Adjust based on your goals and activity level.

Easy protein sources

  • Chicken, turkey, eggs
  • Greek yogurt, cottage cheese
  • Tuna, salmon
  • Beans, lentils, tofu
