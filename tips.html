Tips & Tricks

Small habits that make hitting your protein goal easier.

Easy wins

  • Keep a high-protein “default” breakfast.
  • Pick 2–3 go-to meals you can repeat weekly.
  • Use frozen veggies + pre-cooked proteins when busy.
  • Track protein first, then fill in carbs/fats.

Smart swaps

  • Greek yogurt instead of regular yogurt.
  • Ground turkey or chicken instead of beef sometimes.
  • Protein pasta or chickpea pasta for easy upgrades.
